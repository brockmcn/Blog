One of the biggest things I learned this week was making divs transparent. I used this in my project 1 website to make text boxes hover over images. I have messed around with transparency stuff before, but before I used webkit-filter. For the project and for future, I have learned to just use "opacity: value range(0,1);" with 1 being opaque and 0 being completely transparent. Another thing I learned is how to use a gradient bbackground. A gradient background is essentially just an element that can have multiple colors moving towards a specific direction. For this, I just use the "background: linear-gradient()" function, which you fill with the direction and the colors percentage of where it starts and ends.
